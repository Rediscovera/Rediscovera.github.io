Competition Experience

  • The 10th National University Student Social Practice and Science Contest on Energy Saving & Emission Reduction (National Second Prize) 2016.11 - 2017.08

    • Project Description

      The project is "Carbonless Infinite/Wireless Power Kit". This project developed a set of carbonless infinite/wireless electric energy kit. The purpose of this kit is to solve the problem of short duration of outdoor rechargeable battery and heavy charging equipment, so as to realize the function of charging anytime and anywhere. The main body of the Carbonless Infinite/Wireless Electric Energy Kit consists of a photovoltaic power generation module, a power storage module and a wireless charging module. The photovoltaic charging module converts light energy into electric energy and the storage module stores the excess power. The wireless charging module and the electric energy receiving end complete the transmission of electric energy in the storage module through electromagnetic induction. The exterior of the kit is equipped with Velcro which allows the charging device to be attached in all locations.
